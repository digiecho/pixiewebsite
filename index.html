Pixie Fogging

Release The Pixie's Need your property, business or event sanitised? My Pixie's use the latest fogging techniques, we release a almost invisible mist into the atmosphere, which eventually settles on all surfaces including those impossible to reach by conventional methods. Our disinfectant when used leaves no residue, is non-tainting, non-staining, non-irritant and non-corrosive, leaving surfaces safe and clean for use. Safe for humans, animals, plants and Pixie's. This means that the disinfectant fogging process is particularly suitable as an all surface sanitation process for restaurants, hotels, outdoor events, bars, offices, stores, homes, holiday parks, gyms and vehicles. Used in NHS hospitals and dentists. Regular Pixie fogging , will help prevent the cross-infection of viruses such as MRSA and Covid-19 Corona-virus and many more. We don't just clean, we care....
